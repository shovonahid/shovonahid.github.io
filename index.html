AI in the Classroom:
What It Is Doing to Students
and What We Can Do About It

Read the Research →

Overview

The Question Is Not Whether. It Is What Now.

Picture a student sitting down to do homework and finishing it in minutes without really thinking. They type a question into a chatbot and get a full answer back before they even had a chance to struggle with the problem.

What used to take hours of effort now takes seconds. School starts to feel easy but in a way that feels a little off. That tension is exactly what this project is about. Research shows that AI can genuinely support students in positive ways but it can also quietly chip away at their ability to think for themselves.

As educators the question is no longer whether AI is in our classrooms. It already is. The real question is what we are going to do about it. This site brings together research findings, an interview with a real student, and personal experience to map out where AI helps, where it hurts, and what a better path forward looks like.
