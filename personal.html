First Person Perspective

My Experience With AI as a Student

As a college student, I’ve been using AI as a learning tool for my studies, and I try to use it in a way that supports real understanding rather than just getting quick answers. To me, it would be wasteful to use AI without actually learning what I’m struggling with. Over time, I’ve realized that how you use AI matters just as much as whether you use it at all, since it can either be a shortcut or a tool for deeper learning depending on your approach.

When I’m doing classwork or homework, I sometimes get confused by the material the professor assigns. Instead of immediately emailing the professor, I usually turn to AI to help me understand it. I’ve found that AI often helps me grasp concepts more clearly than a traditional explanation because it tends to walk through the process step by step rather than just giving the answer. This makes it easier for me to actually learn and understand the material.
