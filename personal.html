First Person Perspective

My Experience With AI as a Student

I’m a college student who has been using AI as a learning tool for my studies. To me, it would be wasteful to use AI without actually learning what I’m struggling with. I try to treat it as a way to understand concepts more deeply rather than just getting quick answers. Over time, I’ve noticed that how you use AI matters just as much as whether you use it at all.

In My Own Words

What I Noticed in My Own Learning

When I’m doing classwork or homework, I sometimes get confused by the material the professor assigns. Instead of immediately emailing the professor, I usually turn to AI to help me understand it. I’ve found that using AI often helps me grasp concepts more clearly than a traditional explanation. Rather than simply giving the answer, AI tends to walk through the process step by step, which makes it easier for me to learn and actually understand the material.

— Nahid Shovo, City Tech
